About Task Tracker

Task Tracker

Your Complete Task Management Solution

Task Tracker is a comprehensive task management application designed to help individuals and teams organize their work efficiently. Built with Spring Boot and modern web technologies, it offers a robust platform for tracking tasks, managing priorities, and analyzing productivity.

Easy Task Management
Progress Analytics
Smart Notifications

Key Features

Task Management

Create, update, and track tasks with ease. Set priorities, due dates, and status updates.

Analytics Dashboard

Visualize your productivity with charts and statistics. Track completion rates and trends.

Data Import/Export

Flexible data management with JSON and CSV support for importing and exporting tasks.

Customizable Settings

Personalize your experience with customizable views and notification preferences.
